Flammability Tester

Used to evaluate the flame resistance of a fabric

  • 45 degree flammability tester to check flame resistance of fabrics.
  • Standard stop chord of 50 Tex Resettable Digital TImer for setting time.
  • Special Gas Regulator Specially Designed Gauge for setting flame position.
  • Mild steel construction for long life and aesthetics.

Specifications

Power Supply : 220V.

Timer : Digital.

Accuracy : 1/10th of a second.

Flame Speed : 130mm.

Gauge : 19mm, 16mm, 8mm

Flame Length : 8mm
